I have been playing board games intensively for more than 2 years. I started off with gateway games and gradually advanced to complexer Eurogames. Now I play a variety of board games, including larger Eurogames, shorter card games and party games.

What games I play

Eurogames

Complex games with longer playtime and advanced rules and strategies

Eurogames are some of my favourite games. I've played and greatly enjoyed Brass Birmingham, Underwater Cities, Lorenzo il Magnifico, Dominant Species, Clans of Caledonia, Yamatai, to name a few.

Advanced games

Card games

Smaller games with shorter playtime and usually smaller amount of rules

These games pack a lot of gaming experience into smaller box, shorter playtime and lower price. My favourites include: 7 Wonders Duel, Hanamikoji, Bruxelles 1897, Arboretum.

Shorter games

Gateway games

Simpler rules, great fun to play with anyone

These games offer two significant benefits: they are easy to teach, so you can enjoy them even with non-gamers and increase a chance of lurring new people into the hobby; they usually have quicker play time and don't require to build complex strategies, which makes them great to play even on a regular weeknight when you're tired and don't feel like playing a complex game.

Family fun

Kids games

Games to play with younger audience

As a new generation of prospective gamers is approaching, we need to make sure we have some great games to invite our kids to the hobby. Games that are great at this include: Kingdomino, King of Tokyo, Cat Lady, Century Golem Edition.

Raising future gamers

My favourite games using this mechanic: Agricola, Lorenzo il Magnifico, Dominant Species.

Area control

Visual and cutthroat
My favourite games using this mechanic: El Grande, Dominant Species.
So awesome!

Dice placement

Clever and thinky
My favourite games using this mechanic: Lorenzo il Magnifico, Grand Austria Hotel, Artemis Project.
Tickles the brain!

I learn about new and old games from various sources, including BGG, Board Game Revolution Community on Facebook, YouTube videos made by Rahdo, Slickerdrips, Brettspielblog, GambitTV, Discord channel run by Brettspielblog.

I've learned about many games from friends from my playgroups. That lead me to a lot of game purchases.
